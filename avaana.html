The Company

What does Avaana do?

Avaana is Australia's leading health and wellbeing marketplace — connecting individuals to thousands of practitioners across physiotherapy, massage, counselling, acupuncture, naturopathy, and more. Think of it as the Airbnb for wellness: search by symptom, location, or modality, and book in seconds.

Beyond the consumer product, Avaana runs a B2B vertical — Avaana for Business — which helps companies bring meaningful wellbeing benefits to their teams. This was the frontier I worked on: taking the marketplace model and extending it into the enterprise.
