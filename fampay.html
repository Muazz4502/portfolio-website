The Company

What does FamPay do?

FamPay (by Trio) is India's first neobank built for teenagers — a category that has historically been ignored by traditional financial institutions. It gives teens their own UPI-powered payment account and a numberless FamCard, letting them spend, split, and save independently.

The mission is simple: build a generation that is financially aware, smart, and independent — before they turn 18. FamPay does this through a combination of frictionless payments, parental controls, and financial literacy features embedded directly into the product experience.
