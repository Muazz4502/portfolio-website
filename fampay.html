The Company

India's first neobank for teenagers

FamApp (by Trio) is building the financial infrastructure for a generation that's been overlooked by every bank in the country. We give teenagers a spending account, UPI, and a numberless FamCard, the kind of product that didn't exist when most of us were 14.

The mission is to build a generation that is financially aware, smart, and independent. FamApp does this through frictionless payments, parental controls, and financial literacy features embedded directly into the product.

Payment & Core Banking
UX
Payment Experience Revamp
Unifying two payment rails into a single experience
Redesigned the entire payment flow from the ground up, simplifying the UX while stitching TPAP (bank-linked) and PPI (wallet) payments into a single coherent experience. Users stopped noticing which rail they were on. That was the point.
Product Design Figma TPAP PPI UPI
Regulatory
Success Rate Improvement
Improving acceptance across the UPI wallet ecosystem
Deep data analysis on where and why payments were failing, then took it upstream, working directly with NPCI, RBI, payment aggregators, and merchants to fix acceptance at the ecosystem level. The result: FamApp captured over 75% of wallet UPI market share and was formally recognised by the Reserve Bank of India as systemic in the wallet space.
NPCI RBI Data Analysis Aggregators Merchants
Infrastructure
Payment UX Improvement
Reducing failures before users ever encountered them
Led three systems designed to stop problems before users ever hit them: Killswitch, a circuit breaker that cuts bad traffic in real time; Business Decline Engine, which routes declines intelligently; and Fraud Management System. All three running quietly in the background every time someone pays.
Killswitch Decline Engine Fraud Risk
Performance
Payment Speed
Reducing payment latency across the stack
Pulled multiple levers across the stack: migrated critical payment paths to GoLang, shipped UX changes optimised for perceived speed, and introduced caching for high-frequency flows. Each initiative small on its own. Together, payments got 35% faster.
GoLang Caching Performance Engineering
